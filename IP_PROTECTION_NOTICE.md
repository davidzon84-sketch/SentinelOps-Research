## Scope of This Repository

**SentinelOps-Research** publishes a **Reference Governance Architecture** — the *what* of the Controlled Evaluation Boundary (CEB v1) model: authorized states, transition rules, accountability predicates, audit-evidence schema invariants, and boundary-separation principles suitable for external academic and program review.

This repository is **conceptual research documentation**. It is not software, not a product, and not an operational system.

---

## What Is Not Published Here

**SentinelOps_OS** and related private operational systems constitute separate intellectual property — the *how* of implementation, execution, deployment, and runtime behavior.

That operational IP is **not** in the public domain, **not** licensed under this repository's terms, and **not** included in this publication artifact.

Citation of CEB v1 research materials does **not** grant rights to proprietary implementation, unpublished materials, or operational systems excluded by the boundary model.

---

## Physical and Repository Separation

SentinelOps-Research and private operational repositories are **physically and organizationally separated**. There is **no git connection**, shared history, or automated synchronization between them.

This separation is intentional: it preserves a clean-room boundary between public governance research and proprietary implementation.
